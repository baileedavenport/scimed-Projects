One day in the deep dark woods a girl by the name of Goldilocs was on her way home when she got really hungry and she coudln't wait to eat. She say a little brick house in the distance and yelled with joy. Goldi rushes with full speed on the way to what she thought was destiny. (later to figure out......IT WASN'T)

She slowly walks in, the floor creeply and quietly creeks, she stops in fright and stands still with fear in her eyes. Goldi slowly keeps walking until she comes to what she's been looking for this whole journey...FOOD!!Goldi's fear is quickly replaced with joy and overwelming happiness. There sitting in front of her 3 different sixe bowls full of porridge, and this is where her nightmare begin

Goldi quickly picks up the first bowl it's bigger than the other ones and has more porridge in it, she picks up the ginormous spoon and raises it to her mouth. Less than 5 seconds later she drops the porridge on the ground as she screams "that was TOO HOT" Since she's still hungry she moves on to the next one, this bowl was the smallest of them all. She takes the tiny spoon and raises it to her mouth just like before. Goldi spits it back into the bowl with disgust "this one it too cold"

The last bowl she saw she looked at in aww, it was the perfect amount in a beautiful gold glistening bowl. She slowly and satisfyingly brings the spoon to her mouth and her eye widen in shock, it was some of the best porridge she had ever tasted but also just might be the last.

Goldi roamed the house for a while to try and figure out who it belonged to, but she started to become sleepy and drousy so when she found the beds she hopped from bed to bed, the first one was too big and firm, the second one was too small and soft. Papa bear sees the damage but nothing else, the more he creeps he starts to motion mama and baby bear in. They creep slowy into the house staying far behind papa bear.
